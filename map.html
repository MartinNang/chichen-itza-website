Roar like a mighty jaguar as you explore this temple. The Mayans believed jaguars had special powers. Can you find the jaguar carvings hiding in the stones?

Twist and turn like a spiral shell inside this mysterious tower! The Maya used this as their special sky-watching place. Its windows line up perfectly with the stars and planets. Can you figure out why they named it after a snail?

Peer into the magical waters of this giant natural well! The Maya believed the rain god Chaac lived here. They dropped precious treasures into the water as gifts. Can you imagine what treasures might still be hiding in its depths?
