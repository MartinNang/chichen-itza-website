“El castillo”

Here's your chance to get a super close look at El Castillo, the amazing pyramid at Chichén Itzá! 

Instructions

1. Spin the castle around: Just click and drag your mouse on the model to spin it around.
2. Zoom in and out: Use your mouse wheel to zoom in and out.
3. Move the castle: Click and hold the right mouse button to grab the castle and move it around.
4. Explore different views: See those little squares with arrows on the sides of the model? Click on them to quickly move the castle and see it from different angles.
5. Reset the view: If you want to start over and see the castle from the beginning, just click the "Reset" button. It's like a magic undo button!
