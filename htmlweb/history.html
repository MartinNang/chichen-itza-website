The History of
Chichén Itzá

Play Quiz

2000 BC – 250 AC

Around this time founders of Chichen Itza began to regularly utilize the Sacred Cenote as a pilgrimage site.

495 AC

First Maya settlers built a village and later created settlements with traditional thatched houses.

600 - 1100 AC

Maya and Toltec people built amazing Puuc style temples, the famous pyramid, and played Pok-a-Tok ball game. Chichen Itza became very powerful!

1250 AC

Most families moved away, but some people still came to pray at the magic water hole.

1579 AC

A man from Spain wrote down the name "Chichen Itza" in a book for the first time.

Today

Now Chichen Itza is one of the most famous old places in the world! Lots of people visit every year to see the amazing buildings!
